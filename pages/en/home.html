Introducing the Sébayt Association

Founded in 2018 by PhD students and researchers from Montpellier, the Sébayt Association aims to promote local research, particularly within the Egyptology Institute of Paul-Valéry University Montpellier III.
The association organizes a series of six conferences per year (on Thursday evenings) and a seminar day on a chosen theme one Saturday in February.
